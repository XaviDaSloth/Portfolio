My Projects

As a student and as a newbie developer, I’m excited to share two projects that reflect both ongoing innovation and practical application.

Maritest – Reliability Checking App

Currently in development, Maritest is an app designed to enhance the accuracy and reliability of information. By using advanced algorithms and sentiment analysis, Maritest will evaluate the credibility of various data sources, ensuring users can make informed decisions with confidence. As the app continues to evolve, we aim to create a tool that can be trusted to verify the reliability of content across multiple platforms.

University Queuing System

A project I'm proud to have worked on with my team is the queuing system now implemented at our university. This system efficiently manages student service lines by digitizing the process, reducing wait times, and streamlining operations. It has already shown its value in handling high traffic at key administrative offices, providing a smoother and more organized experience for students and staff alike.
